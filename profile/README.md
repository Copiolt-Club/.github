**Here are some ideas of ticket estimation:**
The Estimation should be always in Fibonacci numbers between 0 to 13 and it can be mesured using fidderent criterias but here we go based on the complexity of developing this. Just imagine how much complex it would be for you to understand this story by going thought the documents, reading line by line and then creating or developing this story. The sample explanations are below

0 - Low or wont do
0.5 - Small task, which might take half and hour to complete it based on complexity
1 - More than small but less than medium task, imagine a task which might take more than half and hour to complete
3 - Medium task, imagine a task which takes 2-3 hours of your time
5 - More than medium less but less than large task, imagine a task which might take more than 3 hours of your time
8 - Large task, imagine a task which might take almost a day
13 - Extra large task, imagine a task which might take more than one day and less than 2 days
